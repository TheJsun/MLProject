Houses by Suburb DatasetOverviewThe Houses by Suburb dataset provides historical data on housing prices for various suburbs across Victoria, Australia. The dataset includes information on the median house prices for each suburb from 2013 to 2023. This data is useful for analysing housing market trends, price fluctuations, and property value appreciation over time in different suburbs.File Name: Houses-by-suburb.csvData Columns1. Locality: The name of the suburb or locality where the house price data was recorded.2. 2013 to 2023: The median house price in the respective year (from 2013 to 2023).3. Change Percentage 13-23: The percentage change in house prices over the 10-year period from 2013 to 2023.UsageThis dataset can be used for various purposes, such as:* Analysing housing price trends over the decade across different suburbs in Victoria.* Comparing the rate of property value appreciation between suburbs.* Assisting potential homebuyers, investors, and real estate professionals in understanding the market conditions and housing demand across regions.* Supporting government planning and development initiatives by identifying housing affordability and growth areas.Missing ValuesSome suburbs may not have data for specific years due to the unavailability of housing sales data in those years. Users are encouraged to handle missing values appropriately, depending on the analysis requirements.
